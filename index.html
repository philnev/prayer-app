Lesson 1: Why Pray?

Prayer is more than words—it’s a relationship. In this lesson, we reflect on why we pray and what it means to bring our whole selves before God.

Scripture Reflection: Luke 11:1 — “Lord, teach us to pray.”

Activity: Spend five minutes writing a letter to God as honestly as you can. Don’t filter your thoughts—just let them flow.

Optional Prompt: What do you hope for when you pray?
