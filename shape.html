SHAPE Course - Full Online Course

Welcome to the SHAPE course. Over six chapters, you'll reflect on your unique calling by exploring your Spiritual Gifts, Heart, Abilities, Personality, Experience, and Next Steps.

Chapter 1: Spiritual Gifts

Every Christian is given spiritual gifts—unique capacities empowered by the Holy Spirit to build up others, glorify God, and express love in action. In this session, we’ll explore what your gifts might be.

Reflective Reading

“Now to each one the manifestation of the Spirit is given for the common good.” — 1 Corinthians 12:7
“Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others.” — 1 Peter 4:10

Guided Assessment

Rate how true each statement is for you using the scale: 0 = Not true at all, 1 = Occasionally true, 2 = Often true, 3 = Very true.

Closing Prayer

God of grace,
Thank you for the gifts you’ve planted in me,

but to serve others, build up your Church,
and reflect your love in the world.
Amen.
