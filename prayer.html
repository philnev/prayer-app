The Prayer Course

Explore eight sessions on different aspects of prayer. Click a tab to watch each session.

Session 1: Why Pray?

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • What do you find difficult about prayer? What do you find easy? Why do you think prayer can often feel hard?
  • What do you mostly talk to God about?
  • Do you feel like you have to act a certain way before God when you pray? Why or why not?
  • Do you find it challenging to persevere in prayer? How can we be encouraged to keep going?

Prayer

God of quiet invitation, help us to keep it simple, real, and faithful. When words fail us, meet us in the silence. Teach us to pray not perfectly but honestly, knowing you love the sound of our voice. Amen.

>

Session 2: Adoration

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • Why do you think that we most often associate prayer with asking, rather than worship and adoration?
  • Do you enjoy spending time with God? When are the times that prayer feels most enjoyable?
  • How does seeing prayer as relational rather than transactional change your perspective?
  • What are the practical things you could do this week to foster an attitude of worship in your daily routine?

Prayer

God of beauty and wonder, lift our hearts to adore you. Teach us joy in your presence and delight in who you are, not just what you give. Shape us to be worshippers in all we do. Amen.

>

Session 3: Petition

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • Do you agree it’s often easier to believe God can change things in our hearts than things we can see in the world?
  • How often do you ask God for what you want as well as what you need?
  • Are you experiencing a 'green', 'amber' or 'red' response to any specific prayers at the moment?

Prayer

Loving Father, you hear our cries before we speak. Give us the courage to ask, the patience to wait, and the grace to accept. In all things, may we trust your loving response. Amen.

>

Session 4: Intercession

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • How do you feel about intercessory prayer – does it come naturally or is it more of a challenge?
  • How does the power of our free will affect your view of intercessory prayer?
  • Have you ever felt called to pray for someone or something? What happened?
  • What would it look like for you to pray this week in alignment with God’s will?

Prayer

God who listens, deepen our care for the world through intercession. May we carry others before you with compassion and courage, praying not to persuade you, but to partner with you. Amen.

>

Session 5: Unanswered Prayer

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • How has the reality of unanswered prayer affected your relationship with God?
  • Have you ever felt God’s silence or absence? How did you respond?
  • How can we become better communities in facing unanswered prayer together?
  • How can we support one another in faithfulness even without easy answers?

Prayer

Suffering God, you are present even in silence. When prayers go unanswered and pain lingers, hold us fast. Grow in us a faithful heart that clings to you in shadow as well as light. Amen.

>

Session 6: Contemplation

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • What is your reaction to the practice of contemplation – does it excite, scare or bore you?
  • How does the quote from Mother Teresa challenge our culture’s attitude to action?
  • What rhythms could you develop to make space for contemplation?

Prayer

Still and gentle God, teach us to be still with you. In the quiet, awaken love. In our pauses, grow presence. May we find you not in striving but in being. Amen.

>

Session 7: Listening

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • Do you feel like your connection to God’s voice is like Wi-Fi, cell phone, or snail mail?
  • How do you experience hearing God’s voice?
  • What practical actions could you take this week to listen more attentively?
  • How can we help each other keep our hearts soft in daily life?

Prayer

God who speaks in whispers and dreams, open our ears and soften our hearts. Help us slow down to hear your voice and trust the unique way you speak to each of us. Amen.

>

Session 8: Spiritual Warfare

Questions for Reflection

  • What did you find most helpful or most challenging in the video?
  • How do you currently approach spiritual warfare? Do you relate to any of the perspectives mentioned?
  • How do you balance awareness of spiritual warfare with a focus on Jesus?
  • How does the call to pray 'your kingdom come' shape your understanding of spiritual warfare?
  • What strongholds do you see in your context and how can you live with a different spirit this week?

Prayer

Mighty God of peace, in the struggle of this world, make us brave and gentle. Teach us to resist evil with love, to pray with boldness, and to walk always in the way of Christ. Amen.
